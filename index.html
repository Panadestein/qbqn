Preamble

Interpreter

The (100 chars) quantum interpreter is based on references arXiv:1711.02086 and arXiv:1608.03355:

  qc

Shor's algorithm

As a test case, we employ the quantum circuit of Shor's algorithm for the number fifteen and base eleven, following references arXiv:1804.03719 and arXiv:2306.09122. The resulting compiled circuit uses five qubits, three of them are control ones. We also define a classical post-processing function: 

n‿a‿qt‿qc‿k ← ⟨15, 11, 5, 3, 2⋆10⟩

s ← ⟨
  ⟨0⟩‿g.h ⋄ ⟨1⟩‿g.h ⋄ ⟨2⟩‿g.h
  ⟨2, 3⟩‿g.cnot ⋄ ⟨2, 4⟩‿g.cnot ⋄ ⟨1⟩‿g.h
  ⟨⟨1, 0⟩, g.P π÷2⟩ ⋄ ⟨0⟩‿g.h
  ⟨⟨1, 2⟩, g.P π÷4⟩ ⋄ ⟨⟨0, 2⟩, g.P π÷2⟩ ⋄ ⟨2⟩‿g.h
⟩

C ← {+‿-{n GCD 𝕩𝕎1}¨ a⋆ (≠÷2×⊑∘⍒) 0⌾⊑+˝∘‿(2⋆qc)⥊𝕩}

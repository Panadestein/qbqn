Shor's algorithm

The resulting compiled circuit uses five qubits, three of which serve as control. To enhance statistical accuracy, the experiment is repeated multiple times. Epilogue

Why BQN, you may ask? Its tacit and functional style together with its support for numerous combinators, which makes programming feel like solving an algebraic problem rather than drafting a political manifesto.
